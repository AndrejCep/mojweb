Professions

3D Modelling

3D modeling is a technique in computer graphics for producing a 3D digital representation of any object or surface. These 3D objects can be either generated automatically or created manually by deforming the mesh, or otherwise manipulating vertices.

Texturing

Texture mapping is a method for defining high frequency detail, surface texture, or color information on a computer-generated graphic or 3D model. Textures are 2D images that are designed to influence a 3D model's appearance.

Animating

Animation is a method in which pictures are manipulated to appear as moving images. In traditional animation, images are drawn or painted by hand on transparent celluloid sheets to be photographed and exhibited on film.
